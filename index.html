Why I Like Reading

My favorite hobby is reading fantasy books. I like books like these because you just get sucked into the story, You just have to know what happens next. Also, you forget about what is happening in the world around you. It is a great way to spend the time. I can finish books quick, so I take more than one. I am in 5th grade but I like books with higher ATOS Book Levels. This is why reading books is my hobby.
